Fortune One

Luxury Farm Plots in Hesarghatta Spread across 4 acres in the serene landscapes of Hesarghatta, Fortune One Essha Vana offers just 18 premium farm plots, starting from 6,000 sq. ft. Located opposite the 356-acre Hesarghatta Grasslands, with the 1,000-acre Hesarghatta Forest and the 1,356-acre Hesarghatta Lakebed nearby, this exclusive development is perfect for those seeking a peaceful retreat amidst nature’s finest surroundings.

Designed for privacy and tranquility, Essha Vana seamlessly blends luxury and nature. Whether you’re looking for a weekend getaway, a farmhouse, or a long-term investment, this is your chance to own a piece of unspoiled beauty within the 5,010-acre Greater Hesarghatta Conservation Reserve. Limited plots available secure yours today!

Nature's Green Heartbeat

Here, life blends seamlessly with the richness of nature. Our thoughtfully designed and preserved greenscapes create a living space that radiates peace and beauty,
nurturing both the soul and the earth. Discover the delight of a home where nature’s bounty is cherished.

Meticulously Crafted Infrastructure Heartbeat

Avenue Plantation

  • Both sides of the road are designed to host larger trees to provide not only aesthetic appeal but also enhanced privacy.

Seamless Walkways

  • Designed to ensure seamless and uninterrupted walking experiences by minimizing level differences.

Three-Tiered Landscape

  • Artfully crafted landscape blending tall and low shrubs along with a tree, bringing the beauty of nature outside every plot.

Vehicular Movement

  • ~5.75-meter-wide concrete driveway accommodating two-way vehicular movement.

Plot Entry

  • A dedicated 4-meter-wide entry to each plot, meticulously designed for effortless access.

Energy Efficient Lighting

  • A combination of solar-powered and electrical streetlights planned at regular intervals.

Chamber-Free Driveways

  • All chambers are thoughtfully tucked below the walkways or landscape areas.

Drip Irrigation

  • A well-planned irrigation system for hassle-free landscape maintenance.

BIODIVERSITY AT HESARAGHATTA GRASSLANDS

Lilac Silverline Butterfly

  • Rediscovered in 2012 after nearly 100 years, this rare butterfly highlights the rich, overlooked biodiversity of the region.

Lesser Florican

  • A globally endangered bird, found only in India, was accidentally sighted in 2012 after a century-long absence.

Onitis Visthara Beetle

  • A newly discovered beetle species, first documented from Hesaraghatta Grasslands in 2023, showcasing its ecological significance.

Peacock

  • Occasionally spotted in these lands, the peacock serves as a reminder of the grassland’s role as a habitat for apex predators.

Slender Loris

  • This nocturnal primate thrives in the wooded habitats, playing a crucial role in maintaining the ecosystem balance.

Why Invest in HESARAGHATTA?

An attractive investment destination.

Current Infrastructures

  • Prime Location with Excellent Connectivity**: 15 km from STRR, 10 km from Namma Metro, and easy access to major highways and metro stations.
  • Close to Kempegowda International Airport**: Only 38 km (approx. 50 minutes) for quick and convenient travel.
  • Thriving Business & Industrial Hubs**: Major tech parks, textile hubs, and the Aerospace & Defense SEZ ensure high growth potential.
  • Eco-Friendly Living**: Surrounded by Hesaraghatta Grasslands and Lakebed, offering stunning views and enhancing air quality.
  • Bren Raceway (20 km)**: A world-class FIA Grade 2 racetrack, creating jobs and boosting the local economy.
